The story of a clay puppet with a great mission

Name: Sparky

Sparky was created with deep love by the old woman Doña Clara, who molded him with magical clay from an ancient river. He was born on a small wooden shelf in Doña Clara's living room, with the secret mission of bringing joy to his owner. His original name was Fulgencio, but his spark of joy made everyone call him Sparky.

Physical Description

Sparky is a clay puppet about 10 centimeters tall. His hair is a mess of red clay spikes, which always seems to be full of energy. He wears a purple volleyball uniform with the number 5 on the front, symbolizing his love for teamwork and collaboration. His eyes, though simple, express infinite kindness and deep empathy. Even though his legs are small and plump, his adventurous spirit does not stop him; he is always ready for a new adventure.

Special Power: The "Spark of Joy"

His power is the "Spark of Joy." Sparky can concentrate all the energy of his heart into a small and bright golden spark. When this spark touches a person, it infuses them with an immediate wave of optimism, erasing sadness and leaving a genuine smile. However, his power only activates when he himself feels deep compassion for the suffering of others.

Unique Characteristics

Aside from his power, Sparky's most special quality is his **resilience**. Although his clay body is fragile and can crack or break easily, Doña Clara always carefully repairs him, which symbolizes that joy can always be restored, even after the most difficult moments. His age is as immeasurable as the joy he has created; he has the age of a kind and wise soul.

What he likes and dislikes

Sparky likes to make people smile, the sound of laughter, the smell of wet earth after the rain, and having quiet conversations with Doña Clara. He dislikes seeing someone sad, prolonged darkness, and excessive noise that prevents him from concentrating to use his power.

Strengths and Weaknesses

His main strengths are his **empathy**, his **determination**, and his inexhaustible power of joy. However, his greatest weakness is his physical fragility, and the fact that he gets overwhelmed by too much sadness at once, which makes him vulnerable and makes it difficult for him to use his power.

Age

He's as old as the joy he created. You could say he's the age of a kind and wise soul.
